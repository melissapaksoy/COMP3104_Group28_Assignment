--Importance of Learning DevOps in the Software Development Cycle--

DevOps plays a critical role in the software development cycle by integrating development and operations to streamline the delivery process. 
Learning DevOps helps developers implement Continuous Integration and Continuous Deployment (CI/CD), enabling faster, more reliable releases. 

It encourages collaboration between teams, improving communication and reducing errors during deployment. 
Knowledge of automation tools, monitoring, and infrastructure management allows developers to anticipate and resolve production issues efficiently.
